The Program

Stratos campaigns primarily single-handed and double-handed offshore distance racing on the US Northeast Coast. The offshore program centers on the Bermuda Race, Annapolis to Newport, Bermuda 1-2, Block Island Race, and the Vineyard Race. Crewed inshore racing runs out of Cedar Point Yacht Club and neighboring clubs on Long Island Sound.
