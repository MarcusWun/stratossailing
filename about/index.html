The Program

Stratos campaigns primarily single-handed and double-handed offshore distance racing on the US Northeast Coast. The offshore program centers on the Bermuda Race, Annapolis to Newport, Bermuda 1-2, Block Island Race, and the Vineyard Race. Crewed inshore racing runs out of Cedar Point Yacht Club and neighboring clubs on Long Island Sound.

Sails

North Sails and Evolution Sails inventory includes a J0 and asymmetrical spinnakers: A1.5, A2, A3, A4.

Owner / Driver

Marcus Wunderlich has been racing sailboats for over 15 years across a wide range of one-design and offshore classes. His background includes competitive campaigns on the J/105, J/70, VX One, Melges 15, and ILCA 7, with participation in numerous local, regional, and national regattas throughout his career.

Stratos represents his move into offshore and shorthanded distance racing. Based at Cedar Point Yacht Club in Westport, CT, Marcus campaigns the Sun Fast 3300 primarily single- and double-handed on the US Northeast Coast.
