Arriving in Newport

Stratos shipped from the UK aboard a Sevenstar cargo vessel, arriving in Newport, RI. After the crane lift and splash, the first stopover was Conanicut Marina in Jamestown for a full systems check before heading home to Liberty Landing in Jersey City.
